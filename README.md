# Team builder for Genshin Impact 

This app helps players make possible teams by taking in the selected characters as input, analysing their elements, abilities and attributes, and outputting desired teams with explanations. It works by assigning a score based on the elemental reactions and the other attributes (e.g Nightsoul) that said team has, and sorting these teams by score. Afterwards, the teams are sent to an LLM for explanation generation.
